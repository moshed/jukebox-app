Jukebox

Your guests pick the music.

Host a session from your Apple Music or Spotify library, share a code, and let everyone vote on what plays next. The most popular songs rise to the top — it's democratic party music.

Apple Music Spotify

How It Works

1

Host a Session

Pick any playlist from your Apple Music or Spotify library. Jukebox generates a unique code your guests can use to join. Share it as a QR code, link, or just tell them the number.

2

Guests Vote

Everyone gets votes to spend on songs they want to hear. The more votes a song gets, the higher it climbs in the queue. When a song plays, votes are returned — so keep voting!

3

The Best Songs Win

No more arguments about what to play next. The crowd decides. Songs with the most votes automatically play first.
